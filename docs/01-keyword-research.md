## 2. Keyword intent map

Three intent layers — every site page should target one primary intent.

| Intent | What the searcher wants | Maps to site section |
|---|---|---|
| **Transactional** | Book a room / check price / compare rates | Rooms, Booking, Offers |
| **Commercial investigation** | "Is this resort right for me?" — reviews, amenities, location | Home, About, Amenities, Reviews |
| **Informational** | Plan a trip — things to do, how to get there, when to visit | Blog / Guides, Location |

## 5. SEO content rules for the build

1. **One primary keyword per page** — never cannibalise. Each URL ranks for one clear query.
2. **Title tag formula:** `Primary Keyword | Brand` (≤60 chars). E.g. `Beachfront Resort Nusa Ceningan | Segara Seaside Resort`.
3. **Meta description:** 150–160 chars, includes primary + a CTA ("Book direct & save").
4. **H1 = primary keyword** (natural phrasing). One H1 per page.
5. **Image alt text** uses secondary keywords — every photo of the villa/pool/beach is an SEO asset.
6. **Internal links** between location↔rooms↔blog to pass authority.
7. **"Book direct & save"** is the recurring conversion CTA (matches their Instagram positioning).
8. **Schema markup:** `Hotel`, `LodgingBusiness`, `Restaurant`, `FAQPage`, `BreadcrumbList`, `AggregateRating` (once reviews are imported).
9. **Local SEO:** consistent NAP (Name/Address/Phone) + Google Business Profile sync.
10. **Long-tail blog cadence:** publish one guide per cluster-C topic to build topical authority.
